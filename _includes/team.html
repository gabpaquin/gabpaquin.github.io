Where to eat?

At Castello di Montegufoni there is a restaurant open for lunch and dinner only every day of the week excluding Tuesday. They have a different set meals every evening consisting of an appetizer, pasta, main and tea and coffee for around €20-€25. As breakfast is not available at the restaurant we do suggest you bring some breakfast food for your mornings at the castle. Your rooms have kitchens with basic kitchen utensils.

There will of course be meals included in the events we will be having for the wedding over the weekend, details to come…
