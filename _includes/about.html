How to get to Castello di Montegufoni

From Florence

Car

  • Exit at Firenze-Scandicci and take the superstrada FIRENZE-PISA-LIVORNO (blue signs) towards Pisa/Livorno.

  • Exit at Ginestra Fiorentina and at the roundabout follow the signs for San Casciano-Cerbaia.

  • At the second roundabout, continue in the direction of Cerbaia. Continue on this road for about 6 km.

  • At Cerbaia junction turn right and continue towards Montagnana.

  • Once you arrive in Montagnana pass the petrol station on your left and after about 500 metres turn right. It is signposted Montegufoni.

  • Continue for about 1 Km and then turn left where you can locate the entrance to the castle of Montegufoni, marked by statues of two lions.

  • This road leads to the parking lot located in front of the Castle, where you can leave your car. The front desk is located inside the Castle, next to the main court.

Bus

You can arrive from Florence by bus via the SITA company line. The bus station is next to the Central Station Santa Maria Novella FS.

Take the bus that has the final destination as Montegufoni Certaldo/Montespertoli and you will need to get off Baccaiano

You will need to walk about 1.2km to get to Castello di Montegufoni from the bus station.
